The culture of Somalia is an amalgamation of traditions in that were developed independently and through interaction with neighboring and far away civilizations. During regular, day-to-day activities, women usually wear the guntiino, a long stretch of cloth tied over the shoulder and draped around the waist. The guntiino is traditionally made out of plain white fabric sometimes featuring with decorative borders
